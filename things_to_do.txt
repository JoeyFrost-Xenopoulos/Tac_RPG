<---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------->
Specifics about combat system
<---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------->
Str: Damage with weapons. Damage = Str + Weapon Mt.

Mag: Damage with Magic. Damage = Mag + Tome Mt.

Skill: Chance to hit and chance to critical. Hit = [(Skill x 3 + Luck) / 2] + Weapon Hit Rate. 
Crit = (Skill / 2) + Weapon's Critical.

Spd: Chance to avoid. Avoid = (Speed x 3 + Luck) / 2. 
Also if your Spd is 5 points higher than the enemy you will attack twice, if your Spd is 5 points lower then they will attack twice.

Luck: Slightly raises Hit AND Avoid. Reduces chance of enemy critical hits. Crit Avoid = Luck

Def: Lowers damage of weapon attacks. Weapon Damage Reduction = Def.

Res: Lowers damage of magic attacks. Magic Damage Reduction = Res.

Consitution: Constitution or physical size affects resuce

Aid: If you unit has lower constituion then can be rescued
